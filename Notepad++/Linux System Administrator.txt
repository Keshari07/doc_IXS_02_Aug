Job Title: Linux System Administrator

Experience Required: 4+ Years
Location: Remote


Job Description:

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Linux System Administrator to join our remote team. The ideal candidate will have over four years of experience in managing Linux systems and possess a strong technical background in various related domains. This role requires a proactive individual who can efficiently manage and optimize systems, troubleshoot issues, and ensure the security and reliability of our infrastructure.


Key Responsibilities:

Operating Systems: Proficiency in Linux and Windows operating systems.
Networking: Understanding of network protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP), network configuration, and troubleshooting.
Scripting and Automation: Experience with scripting languages such as Python, Bash, and PowerShell to automate tasks and improve efficiency.
Security: Familiarity with cybersecurity principles, firewalls, VPNs, and security protocols.
Virtualization: Experience with Proxmox VE and KVM.
System Monitoring and Performance Tuning: Skills in using monitoring tools (e.g., Zabbix, LibreNMS) and optimizing system performance.
Database Management: Basic understanding of database systems (MySQL, MariaDB, PostgreSQL, MS SQL) and their administration.
Configuration Management: Experience with Ansible for managing system configurations.
E-mail Management: Experience with protocols (SMTP, POP3, IMAP) and clients (Microsoft Outlook, Thunderbird, Apple Mail, iOS and Android clients).
Control-panels: Experience with Plesk, DirectAdmin, and cPanel / WHM.
Soft Skills:
Problem-Solving: Ability to diagnose and resolve complex technical issues efficiently.
Communication: Strong verbal and written communication skills to convey technical information clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
Teamwork: Capability to work collaboratively within a team environment and across departments.
Adaptability: Flexibility to adapt to new technologies, processes, and changing business needs.
Attention to Detail: Precision in managing configurations, scripts, and documentation to avoid errors and ensure system reliability.
Time Management: Efficiently managing time and prioritizing tasks to handle multiple projects and deadlines.
Critical Thinking: Ability to analyze situations critically and make informed decisions.
Customer Service Orientation: Willingness to assist and support end-users and clients, ensuring a positive user experience.
Continuous Learning: Commitment to ongoing professional development and staying updated with the latest industry trends and technologies.


Qualifications:

4+ years of experience as a Linux System Administrator or in a similar role.
Proficiency in Linux and Windows operating systems.
Strong knowledge of network protocols, configuration, and troubleshooting.
Experience with scripting languages (Python, Bash, PowerShell).
Familiarity with cybersecurity principles and practices.
Hands-on experience with Proxmox VE and KVM.
Experience using monitoring tools (Zabbix, LibreNMS) and performance tuning.
Basic understanding of database systems and administration.
Experience with Ansible for configuration management.
Knowledge of email protocols and clients.
Familiarity with control panels such as Plesk, DirectAdmin, and cPanel / WHM.
