About the job
Role Summary: The L1 Windows/Linux System Administrator is responsible for providing
first-level support and administration for Windows-based systems. This role involves
troubleshooting basic issues, performing routine maintenance, and ensuring the
stability and performance of the Windows environment.
Key Responsibilities:
• Provide first-level support for Windows-based systems.
• Install, configure, and maintain Windows operating systems and applications.
• Troubleshoot and resolve hardware and software issues.
• Perform routine system maintenance and updates.
• Monitor system performance and ensure compliance with security policies.
• Assist in the setup and configuration of user accounts and permissions.
• Respond to support requests in a timely and efficient manner.
• Document all support activities and resolutions in the helpdesk system.
• Collaborate with other IT team members to resolve complex issues.
• Maintain an inventory of IT assets and manage software licenses.
Required Skills and Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related
field.
• Relevant certifications (e.g., MCSA, CompTIA A+) are preferred.
• 1-2 years of experience in a similar role.
• Proficient in Windows operating systems (Windows 10, Windows Server
2016/2019).
• Basic understanding of networking principles (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP).
• Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
• Excellent communication and customer service skills.
• Ability to work independently and as part of a team.


Preferred Qualifications:

Immediate joiners are highly preferred.
