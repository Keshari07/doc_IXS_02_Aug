About the job
Company Description

Welcome to Adstia, a premier online lead generation and digital marketing agency located in Gurugram. We specialize in helping businesses leverage digital strategies to generate high-quality leads and achieve remarkable growth. With cutting-edge digital marketing expertise and advanced lead generation techniques, we provide tailored solutions to drive targeted traffic, nurture leads, and maximize conversions.


Role Description

Adstia is seeking a dedicated and skilled DevOps Engineer to join our team full-time on-site in Gurugram. In this role, you will play a crucial part in ensuring the seamless deployment and operation of our applications. You will be responsible for implementing infrastructure as code, software development, continuous integration, system administration, and Linux operations.


Key Responsibilities:

Design, build, and maintain scalable and reliable infrastructure using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools.
Develop and manage CI/CD pipelines to automate testing, building, and deployment processes.
Perform system administration tasks to ensure optimal performance and reliability of Linux-based systems.
Manage and optimize cloud infrastructure to support our digital marketing initiatives.
Implement and manage containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
Collaborate with development and operations teams to streamline workflows and improve efficiency.
Monitor system performance and troubleshoot issues to ensure high availability and performance.
